DOM Play

This page uses data-attributes and an anonymous function to loop through a nodelist and compare data-attributes to highlight or unhighlight text. To show this I picked a small section from the script of one of my faviorite movies The Dreams Girls.

C.C.: Effie, Curtis was supposed to?

Effie: Love me! Curtis was supposed to love me!

Curtis: There you are, Effie I've been looking all over.

Effie: I turn my back and find myself out on the line. You could have warned me but that would have been too kind.

Curtis: I've been warning you for months to clean up your act. You've been late, you've been mean, giving all kinds of bull flack.

Effie: That's a lie! That's a lie! It's just I haven't been feeling that well.
